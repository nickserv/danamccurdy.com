Beyond the world of discovery that music has been for me, there was always the bigger world, the universe of questions about what we’re a part of, how it works, and how to best fit into the larger whole.

My love of words, a fascination with scientific, philosophical, and spiritual inquiry, and a quiet determination to learn how to be a better human (and a happier man) have led me on a path of shaping my experience, breath by breath, into poems.

Here are some of them.

life love

Balance

August 30, 2013

Here I sit in tranquility,
Balanced at the edge of everything.
The water laps,
Birds swoop,
Trucks roll by, distant.
Planes boom across the sky.
All is in balance,
Perfect.

On the Spectrum

August 20, 2013

I live on the edges of things,
Drifting at the borders of people’s towns,
Observing their rituals,
Their languages;
Not well enough to speak,
But to capture wisps of meaning,
Hints of intention.

I am my own species,
Co-existing,
Learning to Be.

The “I Am” that I Am

August 13, 2013

The air,
The water,
The sky
Fill me.

Everything that is “me”
Falls away.

This is the “I am”
That I am,
Always.

Dreams

August 11, 2013

Sometimes the gifts of life
Come wrapped in a dream,
And when the dream cracks open,
The gift is exposed:
Not what was wanted,
But what was needed.

This, Now

August 2, 2013

Impaled to the earth,
Cradled in the sky,
I have no past,
I have no future;
I am this,
Now.

Clouds roll by,
Technicolor wonders,
Movies for the camera
Of my soul.

I am this,
Now

I Could say goodbye

July 30, 2013

I could say goodbye
to this room,
I could say goodbye
to this house.
I could say goodbye
to this town,
this country,
this world.

I could say goodbye
to this body,
this life.

What would I be?
What would I say hello to?

The Gifts of Life

July 18, 2013

Sometimes
the gifts of life
come wrapped
in broken dreams.

As the dream dies,
the gift is revealed:
we get what we need,
not what we thought
we wanted..

The truth: 2 snapshots

July 17, 2013

Every disappointment is a gift.
Each failed expectation is an opportunity,
To see, choose, and act differently,
To set sail in a new direction.
Accept your pain, and explore!

When everything looks shitty,
Try looking in a mirror.
If there’s shit on your eyes,
It’s probably yours - wipe it off!

I look up

July 11, 2013

Poised between regret and fear,
I look up,
Breathing,
Into a radiant sky.

I am alive.

Two Moments: Freedom & Coda

July 1, 2013

To fly like the clouds,
amorphous, rootless,
sailing in the sun and wind,
drifting in and out of being.

I look down,
to catch this moment in words;
I look up,
and the bright magic has left the sky,
leaving a darker beauty,
painful to see,
a poem that does not want
to be written;
and still the clouds roll on,
like the sea,
like the earth,
like me.
