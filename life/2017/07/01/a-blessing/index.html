A Blessing

July 1, 2017 life

I bless my body,
as it blesses me.

I bless my spirit,
as it blesses me.

I bless this world,
as it blesses me.

I bless this world’s spirit,
as it blesses me.

I bless our universe,
as it blesses me.

I bless the universal spirit,
as it blesses me.
