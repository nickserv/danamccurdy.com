What Lies Within

August 29, 2014 life

Why do we try so hard to protect ourselves,
when what lies within us
is so powerful,
so complete
and undying?

Why do we protect this illusion,
this veil
that covers our true magnificence,
our birthright.

Why, knowing what is inside,
do we cover it at all?
