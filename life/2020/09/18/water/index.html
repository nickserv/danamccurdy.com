Water

September 18, 2020 life

Light slides shimmering
across the water.

A boat’s wake
slices its surface,
weaving its warp
against the woof
of the rippling wind.

in this quintessent moment,
perfection’s dance
overwhelms my senses,
skewering my thoughts,
bursting the bubble
of my restless mind.
